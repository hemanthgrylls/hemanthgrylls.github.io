Fluid Gallery

The pictures the equations make.

High-speed flow is as much a visual subject as a numerical one. Below are the recurring characters of my work, rendered here procedurally with the same diverging colormap I use for vorticity. Swap any tile for a frame from your own simulations or schlieren.

These six tiles are generated in the browser as placeholders. To use your own media, drop images into assets/gallery/ and replace a <canvas> with <img src="assets/gallery/your-file.jpg" alt="…" />.
