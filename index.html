Dr. Hemanth Chandravamsi is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Johns Hopkins University, specializing in the development of numerical algorithms and high-performance computational tools for solving forward and inverse problems in fluid dynamics. His research addresses fundamental and applied challenges in engineering, with applications spanning aerodynamics, high-speed propulsion systems, and aeroacoustics.
